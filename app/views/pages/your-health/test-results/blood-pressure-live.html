Chart description

This blood pressure chart shows your reading of 140over/90 as a cross in the high blood pressure area.

The vertical line (Y-axis) shows systolic blood pressure numbers. The horizontal line (X-axis) shows diastolic blood pressure numbers.

The chart is divided into 4 areas.

  • Low blood pressure is shown in purple. It ranges from 70over/40 to 89over/59.
  • Healthy blood pressure is shown in green. It ranges from 90over/60 to 120over/80.
  • Slightly raised blood pressure is shown in yellow. It ranges from 121over/81 to 139over/89.
  • High blood pressure is shown in red. It ranges from 140over/90 to 179over/119.

Follow the advice given to you

Follow the advice given to you by the healthcare professional who took your blood pressure reading.

If you start to feel unwell call 111 for advice.

Lowering your blood pressure

Making changes to your lifestyle, like eating healthily and exercising more, can have a positive effect on your blood pressure.

Start by choosing a habit to focus on and get advice on how to make changes.
